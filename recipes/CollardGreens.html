Ingredients

  • 2 large sweet potatoes, peeled and cut into bite-sized chunks
  • 4 bone-in skinless chicken thighs
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons salt
  • 5 tablespoons curry powder, divided
  • 12 large fresh collard green leaves

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 450 degrees F (230 degrees C). Line a baking sheet with parchment or foil, or spray with cooking spray.
  2. Whisk cinnamon, brown sugar, and olive oil together in a bowl; add sweet potatoes and stir until well coated with oil and spices. Place sweet potatoes on the prepared baking sheet in a single layer.
  3. Roast in the preheated oven until tender, 20 to 35 minutes, turning potatoes once halfway through cooking time.
